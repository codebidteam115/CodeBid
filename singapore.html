About Singapore
Singapore is a vibrant island city-state known for its ultra-modern skyline, lush greenery, diverse culture, and culinary delights. It's a global hub for business and leisure, seamlessly blending tradition and innovation.
Top Attractions
  • Marina Bay Sands & SkyPark
  • Gardens by the Bay
  • Sentosa Island
  • Chinatown & Little India
  • Singapore Zoo & Night Safari
Travel Tips
  • Best time to visit: February to April
  • Use MRT and buses with an EZ-Link card
  • Try hawker center food — it’s delicious and affordable
  • Respect local rules — Singapore is clean and well-regulated
