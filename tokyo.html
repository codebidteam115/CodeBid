About Tokyo

Tokyo, Japan’s bustling capital, is a harmonious blend of ultramodern skyscrapers, ancient temples, cherry blossoms, and high-tech living. It is known for its impeccable cleanliness, courteous people, and endless food and fashion choices.

Top Attractions

Sensō-ji Temple
Tokyo’s oldest and most famous Buddhist temple in Asakusa.
Shibuya Crossing
The busiest pedestrian crossing in the world.
Tokyo Skytree
One of the tallest towers in the world with stunning city views.
Meiji Shrine
A tranquil spot dedicated to Emperor Meiji and surrounded by lush forest.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: March–May (cherry blossom season) & October–November
  • Currency: Japanese Yen (¥)
  • Language: Japanese (English signage available in tourist areas)
  • Transport: JR Yamanote Line and Metro are efficient and punctual
