About Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is known for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. The city is home to iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum. Stroll through charming streets, enjoy exquisite cuisine in sidewalk cafés, and soak in the romantic atmosphere.

Top Attractions

Eiffel Tower
Iconic symbol of Paris with breathtaking views from the top.
Louvre Museum
World's largest art museum, home to the Mona Lisa.
Champs-Élysées
Famous avenue lined with luxury shops and cafes.
Sacré-Cœur Basilica
Stunning basilica on Montmartre hill with panoramic views.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: April to June and October to early November
  • Currency: Euro (€)
  • Languages: French (English is widely understood in tourist areas)
  • Public Transport: Efficient metro and bus networks
