About Rome

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a timeless city steeped in history, art, and architecture. Walk through ancient ruins, marvel at Renaissance art, and sip espresso in charming piazzas. Every street in Rome tells a story of emperors, artists, and philosophers.

Top Attractions

Colosseum
The iconic amphitheater symbolizing Roman grandeur and gladiator history.
Vatican City
Home to the Pope, St. Peter's Basilica, and the awe-inspiring Sistine Chapel.
Trevi Fountain
Toss a coin and make a wish at Rome’s most famous Baroque fountain.
Roman Forum
Explore the ruins of ancient Roman government buildings and temples.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: April to June & September to October
  • Currency: Euro (€)
  • Language: Italian (English is widely spoken in tourist areas)
  • Comfortable shoes are a must – lots of cobbled streets!
  • Stay near central Rome for easy access to major sights
