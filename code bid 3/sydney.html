About Sydney
Sydney, the capital of New South Wales, is Australia’s largest city known for its stunning harbor, iconic Opera House, and vibrant coastal lifestyle. With world-class beaches, wildlife parks, and exciting cultural scenes, it’s a must-visit.
Top Attractions
  • Sydney Opera House
  • Harbour Bridge
  • Bondi Beach
  • Taronga Zoo
  • Blue Mountains National Park
Travel Tips
  • Best time to visit: September to November, or March to May
  • Explore using public transport like Opal Card ferries and trains
  • Don't miss a coastal walk from Bondi to Coogee
